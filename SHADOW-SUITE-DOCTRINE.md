# Shadow Suite Product Doctrine

Shadow Suite is a family of defensive security tools with a shared identity but distinct tool personalities. Do not merge tool functionality unless explicitly requested.

The Shadow Throne is the command seat of Shadow Suite. It launches and coordinates tools without modifying their internal functionality.

Preserve the defenders. Improve the castle.


## Shadow Intelligence Fabric

Shadow Throne consumes evidence generated by individual Shadow Suite tools. Reports, logs, exports, and future Shadow Intelligence JSON companions remain owned by the tool that generated them. Shadow Throne indexes this evidence, creates a read-only intelligence snapshot, and surfaces high-level attention areas without replacing or modifying the detailed tool reports.

The intelligence model must remain scalable toward an enterprise app and future AI agent:

- Tools remain authoritative.
- Shadow Throne indexes and summarizes evidence.
- Shadow Throne does not perform uncontrolled remediation.
- Future AI/agent features should read structured evidence, explain findings, recommend next actions, and require human approval before execution.
